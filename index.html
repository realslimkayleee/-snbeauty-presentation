Current SEO Performance

Here is a plain-English breakdown of your website's current search performance. Each score measures a different aspect of your site, explaining what it means for your business.

Overall Grade:

C+

This is the big-picture score for your website's search performance. A C+ means you've got a solid foundation, but there are some important gaps keeping you from ranking as high as you could. Most are very fixable.

GEO (AI Readiness)
Grade: F

What it measures:

How well your website is set up to be found by AI tools like ChatGPT and Google's AI answers.

Your score:

Needs the most attention. Your site is missing the behind-the-scenes information that tells these AI tools who you are, that you're a med spa, where you're located, and what you offer — so you may be getting skipped when people search this way.

Usability
Grade: A

What it measures:

How easy and pleasant your website is to use, especially on phones and tablets.

Your score:

Excellent. Your site looks great and works smoothly across all devices, which is exactly where most of your customers will be viewing it. Nothing to worry about here.

Performance
Grade: B+

What it measures:

How quickly your website loads and how smoothly it runs for visitors.

Your score:

Very good. Your pages load fast, so visitors aren't left waiting and clicking away. A few minor behind-the-scenes adjustments could make it even quicker, but you're in strong shape.

On-Page SEO
Grade: B

What it measures:

How well the actual content and setup of your page tells Google what your business is about.

Your score:

Good. Your page headlines, descriptions, and structure are mostly in good shape. A few small tweaks — like a longer page title and descriptions on your photos — would push this even higher.

Links
Grade: F

What it measures:

How many other trusted websites link to yours. Search engines treat these like recommendations.

Your score:

Needs work. This is the biggest area for improvement. Very few quality websites currently link to yours, which holds back your ranking. Getting listed in trusted local and industry directories is the single most valuable step we can take.
